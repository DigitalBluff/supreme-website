Our History

Supreme Urban Logistics leverages 40 years of experience in trucking, warehousing, and expedited delivery, specializing in New York City and the tri-state area. We navigate time-sensitive deliveries, complex traffic, building restrictions, and last-mile needs using advanced routing and real-time tracking technology.

With a skilled team and a fleet equipped for everything from lightweight parcels to heavyweight furniture, Supreme Urban Logistics delivers tailored solutions with street-smart expertise that only comes from decades of operating in America's most demanding delivery markets.

We understand one crucial truth: urban logistics isn't just logistics in a city — it's an entirely different discipline. And we've mastered it.
